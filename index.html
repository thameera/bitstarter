Everyone has got a favorite blogroll. You don't want to miss any update but you don't want to get disturbed in the middle of the day either. OneMail sends you a daily email with a summary of the new items in your favorite blogs.


Why choose OneMail over a popular feed reader?

  • You forget to open your reader everyday. You never forget to check your mail.
  • Most blogposts are just noise. With OneMail you just get a summary and you can choose which to read.
  • No unread counts. Because you never miss an important article.

Features

FAQ

What is the business model of OneMail?
OneMail will be a freemium service. Free users will be able to subscribe to a limited number of feeds. Premium users will be able to subscribe to unlimited feeds, configure email format, configure email delivery time and enjoy a load of other premium features.
Why do you need funding?
A feed aggregator requrires a lot of resources and hosting power to operate, and these cost quite a bit of money. We need funding to acquire enough hosting resources to provide a smoothly-running service.
